Welcome to Thing Finder!

Thing Finder is the hugely necessary new app by Craig Dickson which allows you to remember where you put your things.

All you have to do is visit this website every time you put anything anywhere, and add (or update) the details of the thing that you will later wish to find.

Truly, the optimal solution!
